About Me

I am a strategic writer, communicator, and mission driven professional with more than 10 years of experience managing the regional and national strategic communications to further the missions of for profit and non-profit organizations.

These experiences, combined with my MBA degree and my undergraduate degree in Journalism, have allowed me to appreciate a variety of innovative, challenging environments where I thrive as a professional. My knowledge includes work in areas such as: healthcare, wellness, change management, natural products, finance, print media, online media, retail, NGO - environmental issues, pet nutrition, energy, and sustainable business.
